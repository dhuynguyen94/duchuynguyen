About me

I am Nguyen Duc Huy (Nguyễn Đức Huy) , a Software Engineer in the Sensor Intergrated Circuit Laboratory (Sensor IC Lab - SIL) at National Chiao Tung University (NCTU),Taiwan. I received Bachelor Degree - B.S in Electrical and Automation Engineering from Danang University of Science and Technology (Đại Học Bách Khoa Đà Nẵng) , and then I received Master Degree - M.S in Computer Science from " National Chiao Tung University (國立交通大學, NCTU) which is be replaced with the name National Yang-Ming Chiao Tung University” (國立陽明交通大學, NYCU) . My current research interests lie in applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning for Blood Flow Volume prediction using a hand-held biomedical device. In additional, I am also working for Embedded System deveploed on NUCLEO-F767ZI platform via using C++ Programming Language , which is applied for this hand-held biomedical device. In my leisure time, I love to play football and go swimming.
